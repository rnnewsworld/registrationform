Salman Khan Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan Box Office Collection Day 3

ByRN NEWS WORLD April 24, 2023April 24, 2023

Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan Box Office Collection Day 3: Salman Khan’s movie Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan gained power at the box office in its first weekend after a slow Eid opening. In only three days, the movie raked in more over Rs 66 crore.

Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan Box Office Collection Day 3: Only Rs 15.81 crore was made by Salman Khan’s April 21 release, Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan, in its first day. Additionally, it had the second-lowest first-day opening of the superstar’s 5 Eid flicks. Although it was disappointing to see a Salman Khan-starring movie earn so much on Day 1, the movie managed to make up for it throughout the course of its first weekend in theatres. On the second day, KKBKKJ saw a surge and made Rs 25.75 crore. The movie earned in a stunning Rs 66.25 crore on Day 3, April 23, a Sunday.

Salman Khan and Pooja Hegde play important characters in Kisi Ki Jaan. The movie was the star’s first significant theatrical release following Dabangg 3. KKBKKJ had incredible growth in their collection throughout the first weekend after a slow start on Day 1. The third day brought in Rs. 26.25 crore, bringing the total to Rs. 66.25 crore. On Sunday, April 23, KKBKKJ had a 28.56 percent total Hindi occupancy. Ajith-starring Veeram, which was a box office hit at the time, has been recreated for Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan, which is marketed as a mass-market family film.

Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan Box Office Review:

Although reviewers gave Salman Khan’s Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan bad or mixed reviews, the movie did well at the box office. The movie made 25 crore over the Eid holiday on Saturday and earned same numbers on Sunday after a considerably lower start of 15 crore on Friday. The current total is around 66.5 crore.
